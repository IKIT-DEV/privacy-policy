Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 24, 2025

1. Data we collect

Our app does not collect, store on our servers, or share any users’ personal data with third parties.
The app does not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.

2. QR code scanning

The app processes QR code content only on the user’s device.
At the user’s request, the result can be saved locally on the device.

3. Device access

The app may require access to the camera to scan QR codes. Camera data is not sent to us and is not stored by us.

4. Sharing data with third parties

We do not share data with third parties.

5. Data storage and deletion

QR codes saved by the user are stored locally on the device. You can delete them in the app (or by clearing the app’s data / uninstalling the app in your device settings).

6. Children

The app does not collect personal data from children or adults. If you believe any data has been collected by mistake, please contact us.

7. Contact

For privacy questions: service@ikit.dev
